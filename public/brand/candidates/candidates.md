# makerportal.ai — Chosen Brand Identity Concept

After extensive iteration across 20+ candidate concepts, we have selected the **Mid-Card Dot** (`agm`) as the new brand identity icon for `makerportal.ai`.

---

## Chosen Concept: Mid-Card Dot (`agm`)

### The Design
Three angled app cards tilted at progressive angles (-14°, -7°, 0°). Each card carries a crimson indicator dot — the back card's dot sits at the right edge (21, 18), the middle card's dot at center-right (20, 14), and the front card's dot dead-center (16, 16). Together they form a visual trail from right to center.

### Hover Animation
All three cards snap to 0° rotation and align. The three crimson dots independently animate to a single unified point at (16, 16) — the exact landing position of the lockup period dot when it slides from the wordmark into the icon.

### Why This Concept
- **Dot choreography**: The per-card dots form a trail that mirrors the lockup dot's trajectory from the wordmark period into the icon core
- **Angled layers**: The progressive -14°/-7°/0° tilt creates depth and motion without clutter
- **Modular feel**: Three cards evoke the multi-app hub identity of makerportal.ai
- **Clear at all sizes**: Reads well from 16px favicon to full lockup
