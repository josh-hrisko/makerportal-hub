# Walkthrough — makerportal.ai Brand Identity (Mid-Card Dot Selected)

## Final Selection: `agm` — Mid-Card Dot

After iterating through the App Stack edition candidates and the Angled Layers family, the **Mid-Card Dot** concept has been selected as the new brand identity for makerportal.ai.

## What Changed

1. **Default concept**: `body:not([data-brand-concept])` now shows `.concept-agm-path` (was `.concept-cnc-path`)
2. **Core dot hidden for agm**: The standalone crimson core dot is suppressed — the three per-card dots carry the crimson identity
3. **Dot choreography**: Three card-dots trail right→center (21,18 → 20,14 → 16,16) and all converge to (16,16) on hover, matching the lockup dot transition target
4. **Page copy updated**: Hero, showcase, playground, and app icon sections all reference Mid-Card Dot
5. **Documentation refreshed**: `candidates.md` rewritten to reflect the chosen concept and angled-layers family

## How the Dot Choreography Works

- **Rest state**: Three cards at -14°/-7°/0° tilt, each with a crimson dot at a progressively inward position
- **Hover**: Cards snap to 0°, all three dots animate independently to cx=16 cy=16
- **Lockup integration**: The lockup dot slides from the wordmark period into (16,16) — the same center point all card dots converge to

## Available on /brand

The brand playground at `/brand` shows the full Angled Layers family (11 variants) plus the original 4 App Stack candidates. The active/default concept is `agm`.
